Overview

PEGA has become one of the leading low-code application development and business process management platforms used by organizations to automate workflows, improve customer engagement, and optimize operational efficiency. As businesses continue investing in digital transformation initiatives, demand for professionals with PEGA skills continues to grow. PEGA training helps individuals understand application development, workflow automation, case management, decisioning strategies, and enterprise process optimization required in modern business environments.

This training program provides a comprehensive introduction to PEGA platform capabilities, application development methodologies, workflow design, and automation techniques. Participants gain practical knowledge of how enterprise applications are designed, implemented, managed, and optimized to support business operations. Whether you are beginning your PEGA journey or preparing for PEGA certification, this training provides the foundation needed to build confidence and advance your career.

PEGA - What You Will Learn

  • Core PEGA Concepts and Platform Fundamentals

    Understand PEGA architecture, low-code development principles, application lifecycle management, and the core capabilities that power enterprise automation solutions.

  • Design and Implement Scalable PEGA Applications

    Learn how to create applications that support business processes, workflow automation, customer engagement, and operational efficiency requirements.

  • Apply Best Practices for Automation and Digital Transformation

    Explore PEGA development methodologies and learn techniques used to improve productivity, customer experiences, and business outcomes.

  • Workflow Automation, Case Management, and Decisioning Strategies

    Understand case management principles, business process automation, decision rules, customer engagement frameworks, and workflow optimization techniques.

  • Effective Approaches for Application Modernization and Business Process Improvement

    Learn how organizations use PEGA solutions to automate processes, modernize applications, and improve operational performance.

PEGA Certified System Architect (CSA)

The PEGA Certified System Architect (CSA) course provides a comprehensive introduction to PEGA application development concepts, workflow automation, case management principles, and platform fundamentals. This course is ideal for individuals looking to develop foundational PEGA knowledge and prepare for the PEGA Certified System Architect certification exam.
